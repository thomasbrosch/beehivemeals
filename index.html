Goal / Spend modes use the full funnel (sessions × conversion × new-customer share). Spend × CPA skips the funnel and uses the direct identity New Customers = Spend ÷ CPA — useful when you'd rather plan around a CPA you can actually hit. At your current ~$145 CPA, $60K buys ~414 new; at the $85 goal it would buy ~706. The gap between those columns is the prize for fixing conversion, creative, and tracking. Companion to the Excel model's Goal Planner, Funnel Calculator, Spend Spectrum and Spend x CPA Matrix tabs.
